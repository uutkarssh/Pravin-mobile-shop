Our Story

About Pravin Mobile Shop

Pravin Mobile Shop was founded with one mission: to bring premium-quality mobile accessories to the people of Bhadohi at honest, fair prices. Located in the heart of Subhash Nagar Market, Bhadohi, Uttar Pradesh, we have proudly served over 5,000 local customers since our founding.

From budget-friendly covers to high-end wireless earbuds, our carefully curated collection spans every smartphone brand and model. Every product in our store is hand-picked for quality, durability, and value.
